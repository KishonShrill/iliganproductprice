# Contributing & Ranking Up

Budget Buddy thrives on accurate data. Every time you submit a new price or verify an existing one, you are actively helping other shoppers save money. 

## Best Practices for Submitting Prices

To keep the platform reliable, please follow these guidelines when adding to the Community Hub:

1.  **Check the Date:** Ensure the price you are submitting is current. Do not submit prices from old receipts.
2.  **Accurate Locations:** Double-check that you are attributing the price to the correct store branch.
3.  **Clear Photos:** If uploading a product image or receipt proof, make sure the text and price tag are clearly legible.
4.  **Exact Names:** Include the brand, product name, and size/weight (e.g., *Energen Vanilla 30g* rather than just *Energen*).

## How the Voting System Works

Every submission goes to the **Community Hub** under the "To Review" tab. 
* **Upvotes (👍):** Give an upvote if you have recently seen the item at that exact price in that store.
* **Downvotes (👎):** Give a downvote if the price is outdated, the item is no longer stocked, or the details are incorrect.

*Note: You have a limited number of votes and submissions per day to prevent spam.*

## Ranking Up 🏆

Active and accurate contributors earn trust within the platform. As you build a track record of reliable submissions, you can rank up:

* **Regular User:** Can submit prices and vote on pending items (up to the daily limit).
* **Moderator:** Earned by consistently providing accurate data and identifying incorrect prices. Moderators have higher daily limits and can directly approve or reject community submissions.
* **Admin:** Platform managers who oversee the entire database and user clearances.
