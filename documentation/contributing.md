# Contributing & Ranking Up

Budget Buddy thrives on accurate data. Every time you submit a new price or verify an existing one, you are actively helping other shoppers save money. 

## Best Practices for Submitting Prices

To keep the platform reliable, please follow these guidelines:

1. **Check the Date:** Ensure the price you are submitting is current. Do not submit prices from old receipts.
2. **Accurate Locations:** Double-check that you are attributing the price to the correct store branch.
3. **Clear Photos:** If uploading a product image or receipt proof, make sure the text and price tag are clearly legible.
4. **Exact Names:** Include the brand, product name, and size/weight (e.g., *Energen Vanilla 30g* rather than just *Energen*).

## How the Voting System Works

Every submission goes to the **Community Hub**. A submission requires **10 total votes** to be processed. If it maintains at least a **50% approval rating**, it is approved and published!
* **Upvotes (👍):** Give an upvote if you have recently seen the item at that exact price.
* **Downvotes (👎):** Give a downvote if the price is outdated or the item doesn't exist there.

## Earning XP & Rewards 💰

Instead of just rewarding submissions, we reward *accuracy*. You earn Experience Points (XP) based on how helpful your contributions are:

* **Contribution Approved:** +25 XP
* **High-Quality Approval (80%+ upvotes):** +10 Bonus XP
* **Contribution Rejected:** +5 XP (Thanks for trying!)
* **Casting a Vote:** +1 XP
* **Accurate Vote Bonus:** +2 XP (Awarded if the item's final outcome matches your vote).
* **Vote Session Complete:** +10 XP (Awarded for using all your daily votes).

*Note: To prevent abuse, you cannot vote on your own submissions, duplicate votes are blocked, and daily submission/vote limits are strictly enforced.*

## Ranking Up 🏆

As you earn XP, your community title automatically upgrades:

* **Budget Starter (0 XP):** Welcome to the community! You can submit prices and vote up to your daily limits.
* **Wise Spender (100 XP):** You are getting the hang of it. Your votes carry weight.
* **Budget Guru (300 XP):** You are a highly trusted community member and local market expert.
* **Moderator:** Hand-picked from our active Budget Gurus. Moderators have access to the Console, bulk-upload features, and can bypass the voting queue.
* **Admin:** Platform managers who oversee the database and roles.
