# Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need an account to use Budget Buddy?
You can browse stores and view prices without an account. However, if you want to use the Cart feature, submit new prices, or vote on the Community Hub, you must log in or create an account.

### How do I know the prices are accurate?
Prices are crowdsourced and verified by the community. A price must receive a specific number of upvotes (usually 10) in the Community Hub before it is officially published to the main store listings.

### I saw a wrong price, what should I do?
If a published price is outdated, you can submit a new, updated price for that item. Once the community verifies your new submission, it will replace the old price. 

### Why can't I submit any more prices today?
To ensure quality control and prevent spam, regular users are limited to a certain number of price submissions and votes per day (e.g., 5 votes and 1 submission). Your limits will reset at midnight.

### How can I become a Moderator?
Moderator status is granted to users who have a long history of accurate price submissions and helpful community votes. Keep contributing quality data, and the admin team will upgrade your clearance level!
