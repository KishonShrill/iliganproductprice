# Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need an account to use Budget Buddy?
You can browse stores, view price history, and use the cart without an account. However, to see the latest listings in [budget hub](/budget-hub), submit new prices, vote on the [Community Hub](/contribution/hub), or earn XP to rank up, you must log in.

### How do I know the prices are accurate?
Prices are crowdsourced and verified. A price must receive a total of **10 votes** with at least a **50% approval rating** in the Community Hub before it is officially published.

### How do I check if a price has gone up recently?
Just click the product card. Clicking it will open a Price History chart showing all the previous prices recorded for that item at that specific location.

### I saw a wrong price, what should I do?
If a published price is outdated, you can submit a new, updated price for that item in the [Community Hub](/contribution/hub). Once the community verifies your new submission, it will replace the old price and the old price will be logged in the item's history.

### Why can't I submit or vote anymore today?
To ensure quality control and prevent spam, regular users are limited to a certain number of daily votes and submissions. Completing your daily vote session actually earns you a +10 XP bonus! Limits reset at midnight.

### How can I become a Moderator?
Moderator status is granted to trusted users. First, you need to earn enough XP through accurate submissions and votes to reach the **Budget Guru (300 XP)** rank. From there, the admin team regularly reviews Gurus to upgrade their clearance level!
